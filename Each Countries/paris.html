Welcome to Paris!

What we will do in Paris?

Whats so special in these places??

Eiffel Tower

Day(1-2)

Unveil the magic of the Eiffel Tower on your Paris escapade. With stunning panoramic views, this iconic landmark, a symbol of romance and French heritage, promises an unforgettable experience. Whether marveling at the city from its heights or soaking in its elegance from below, the Eiffel Tower is a must-visit gem, adding timeless charm to your travel adventure.

Louvre Museum

Day(3-4)

The Louvre Museum in Paris is a must-visit for its unparalleled collection of over 35,000 artworks spanning centuries. Home to iconic masterpieces like the Mona Lisa, it offers a captivating journey through human creativity. Set in an opulent historic palace, the Louvre provides a unique and immersive experience in art and culture, making it an essential destination for any traveler.

Notre Dame Cathedral

Day(5)

Notre Dame Cathedral, a mesmerizing Gothic masterpiece in Paris, enchants visitors with its timeless allure. Despite restoration after the 2019 fire, the cathedral's stained glass, flying buttresses, and iconic gargoyles continue to captivate. Ascending its towers reveals panoramic views, and exploring its rich history dating back to the 12th century makes Notre Dame a symbol of cultural and spiritual significance at the heart of Paris.

Champs Elysées / Arc of Triumph

Day(6)

The Champs-Élysées and the Arc de Triomphe epitomize Parisian elegance. The famous avenue, lined with theaters and boutiques, leads to the majestic arch commissioned by Napoleon. The arch offers stunning city views, commemorating France's history and sacrifices, making this duo a must-see for a taste of Parisian grandeur.

Palace of Versailles

Day(7-8)

The Palace of Versailles, a UNESCO World Heritage site near Paris, is a dazzling showcase of opulence and history. Built during Louis XIV's reign, it boasts lavish interiors, including the famed Hall of Mirrors, and expansive gardens. A visit offers a glimpse into the grandeur of 17th-century French monarchy

Disneyland Paris

Day(9-10)

Disneyland Paris, a magical resort just outside the city, invites visitors of all ages into the enchanting world of beloved Disney characters and stories. With two theme parks, including iconic attractions and lively entertainment, it offers a joyous escape filled with the magic of Disney
